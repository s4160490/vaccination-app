Our Mission & Purpose

VaxInsight is a public health data platform built to make WHO immunisation data accessible, understandable, and actionable for a diverse range of users.

How We Address the Challenge

Vaccine-preventable diseases remain a major global health challenge. Despite decades of progress — including the eradication of smallpox and near-elimination of polio — disparities in vaccination coverage across countries and economic groups persist.

VaxInsight aggregates WHO data and presents it through a clear, user-centred interface, enabling both high-level summaries and deep data exploration without requiring technical expertise.

Our goal is to inform, not advocate — presenting data in an unbiased, respectful, and accessible way so that every stakeholder can draw evidence-based conclusions.
